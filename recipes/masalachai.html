Masala Chai

This chai is hot and flavorful thanks to its use of cardamom, clove, and peppercorn

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sugar
  • 1 whole cardamom pod
  • 1 whole cardamom pod
  • 2 black peppercorns
  • 2 teaspoons black tea leaves
  • ½ cup warm milk

How to make masala chai

  • Combine the water and sugar in a small saucepan, and bring to a boil.
  • Add the cardamom pod, clove, peppercorns and tea leaves.
  • Remove from heat, and let the mixture steep for 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Strain into a cup, and fill cup the rest of the way with milk.
  • Sit back, relax, and enjoy!
